Welcome to Project // Typings

Project // Typings is a site about keyboards for enthusiasts, made by enthusiasts.
This site welcomes anyone from newcomers who are interested to those who have experience.
We have an ever-growing community and compilations of different guides and mods that you can use.
All of this and more, for free.

We also accept keyboard commissions for those who are new or those who don't have enough time to build.
We have partnered communities led by fellow enthusiasts who share the same passion we do.
Now go, show the world what you can do.
